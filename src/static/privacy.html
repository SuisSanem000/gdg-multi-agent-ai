Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy clarifies how session data, contact vectors, and queries are handled by the GDG Yerevan AI Workshop prep application.

1. Scope and Local Processing

This application is designed to run locally or inside standard container platforms (such as Google Cloud Run). Most processing occurs dynamically during execution:

  • The contact data search matches are retrieved from your locally initialized in-memory database.
  • Session variables (such as facts stored by the coach agent) are kept in ChromaDB session memory.
  • We do not transmit your local contact records or user chat queries to any external database hosted by the software developers.

2. Google Cloud Vertex AI Integration

By using this application, queries, contact parameters, and chat histories are transmitted to the **Google Cloud Vertex AI (Agent Platform)** endpoints under your configured service credentials. These request payloads are processed by Google's generative models (e.g. gemini-2.5-flash) to calculate tool arguments and draft relationship coaching strategies.

All data sent to Vertex AI is subject to Google's standard enterprise terms of service and data security commitments. Enterprise model requests are not used to train base models.

3. Data Control and Memory Clearance

You have full control over the saved relationship facts in this application. At any time during your workshop testing, you can click the Clear Memory button in the top navigation panel. This will immediately purge all session variables stored in ChromaDB for your active Session ID.

4. Contact Us

For any questions regarding this workshop project and data configurations, please contact the workshop team during the interactive session.
